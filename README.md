# NACOS-LASU Awards — E-Voting Platform

The official voting platform for the NACOS-LASU Awards at Lagos State University. Built to make voting simple, secure, and accessible for every student.

---

## What It Does

Students visit the site, browse award categories, select their nominees, and pay securely to cast their votes. Admins manage everything from a protected dashboard.

---

## For Voters

1. Go to [nacoslasuawards.vercel.app](https://nacoslasuawards.vercel.app)
2. Click **Start Voting** or **Explore Polls**
3. Pick a category and choose your nominees
4. Add votes to your cart
5. Enter your email and pay with Squad
6. Your votes are recorded instantly

You can vote in multiple categories in one payment — just add them all to your cart before checking out.

---

## For Admins

Log in at the **Admin** button in the top right corner.

From the admin dashboard you can:

- Add and manage award categories
- Add nominees to each category
- Open or close voting per category
- View live vote counts and results
- Export results as CSV or JSON
- Change your admin password

---

## Pricing

Each vote costs the amount set per category (e.g. ₦100 per vote). A small ₦50 platform fee is added at checkout regardless of how many categories you vote in.

---

## Security

- Payments are processed securely by **Squad** (GTBank/HabariPay)
- Votes are only recorded after payment is confirmed
- The admin panel is password protected
- All data is stored securely on Firebase
